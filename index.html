Menu

Big Pete’s House of Munch is a family-style restaurant at the heart of Quahog, Rhode Island. Founded in 2009 by local icon, "Family Guy," and entrepreneur Peter Griffin, we’ve built our reputation on serving classic American comfort food at honest, affordable prices.

Our menu celebrates the best of New England and American cuisine—blending traditional favorites with fresh, modern touches. We take pride in using seasonal ingredients and baking fresh bread in-house every day, ensuring every meal is as satisfying as it is memorable.

At Big Pete’s, we believe good food is best enjoyed with good company. Whether you’re here for a casual bite or a family gathering, our warm hospitality and welcoming atmosphere make every visit feel like home.
